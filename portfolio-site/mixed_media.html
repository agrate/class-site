Amanda Grate

For one of my graphic design classes we were challenged to create something 5 days a week in the spring of 2020. At this time I was really drawn to creating soemthing physical due to being on zoom for classes. I tried to use things that would have just been thrown out and use that as a main practice of mine. I reused parts of brown bags, receipts, magazines, and other fun clippings. Togheter I paired these elements with paint, oil pastels, pen, and colored pencil for texture and color variation. I created many other types of collages, but these are my strongest of them all.
