Amanda Grate

For one of my graphic design classes, in the spring of 2020, we were challenged to create something 5 days a week. This was my creation one day. I started an abstract painting and kept adding layer after layer. After being satisfied with the amound of layers and color combinations I decided to cut the big painting into fourths. I then photographed each seperate piece and brought them into illustrator to create a digital version of these paintings.
