Amanda Grate

These photos were captured in the winter of 2020 at Providence Canyon State Park, a little south of Columbus, Georgia. It's often referred to as the Grand Canyon of the south. I visited the park while at my parents house in Montgomery, AL over Christmas break. There are 9 canyons in the state park and I went through each one. Together these are my best photos. I spent the day exploring little nooks of each canyon and went beyond the normal hiker.
